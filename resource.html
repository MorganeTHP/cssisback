Flash Deep is a rock festival set amongst 200km of stunning Pyrenean pistes. With an unparalleled backdrop of breathtaking wilderness, enthusiasts can enjoy freshly powdered ski runs and daringly exciting snowboarding slopes.

As the day blends into night, the festival erupts into a celebration of all things rock, bringing in some of the most exciting bands to electrify the slopes.

Already confirmed for the festival this year are the likes of Wolfmother, Skindred, Zebrahead, Northcote, Matze Rossi and Jon Snodgrass.
